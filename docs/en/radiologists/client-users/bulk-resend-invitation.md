# How to Bulk Resend Invitations to Users

Learn how to resend pending invitations to multiple clinic users at once on the UniRadio platform.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Access the client list

In the side menu, click on **"Clients"** to view the registered clinics.

![Clients](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/01-clients.jpeg)

### 3. Select the clinic

Click on the clinic whose users you want to manage.

![Select clinic](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/02-select-clinic.jpeg)

### 4. Access the account users

Inside the clinic, click on the users tab to view the registered users.

![Account users](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/03-users-account.jpeg)

### 5. Select the desired users

Check the selection boxes of the users to whom you want to resend the invitation.

![Select users](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/04-select-users.jpeg)

### 6. Click the actions dropdown

With the users selected, click the bulk actions dropdown menu.

![Actions dropdown](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/05-dropdown.jpeg)

### 7. Select "Resend Invitation to Selected Users"

In the menu, choose the **"Resend Invitation to Selected Users"** option.

![Resend invitation](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/06-resend-invite.jpeg)

### 8. Click "Execute Action"

Confirm the operation by clicking **"Execute Action"** to resend the invitations to the selected users.

![Execute action](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/bulk-resend-invitation/07-executar-acao.jpeg)
