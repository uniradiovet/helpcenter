# How to Edit a Client Account User

Learn how to update the information and permissions of a user linked to a client account on the UniRadio platform.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Access "Clients"

In the side menu, click on **"Clients"** to view the list of registered clients.

![Clients](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/edit-user/01-clients.jpeg)

### 3. Select the clinic

Click on the clinic to open its management page.

![Select clinic](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/edit-user/02-select-clinic.jpeg)

### 4. Access "Account Users"

Inside the client profile, click on **"Account Users"** to view the users linked to it.

![Account Users](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/edit-user/03-users-account.jpeg)

### 5. Select the user

Click on the name of the user you want to edit.

![Select user](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/edit-user/04-select-user.jpeg)

### 6. Update the information

Edit the desired fields, such as the user's name, role, or permissions.

![Update information](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/edit-user/05-update-info.jpeg)

### 7. Click "Save"

After making the changes, click **"Save"** to confirm them.

![Save](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/edit-user/06-save.jpeg)
