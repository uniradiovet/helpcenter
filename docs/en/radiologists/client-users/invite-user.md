# How to Invite a User to a Client Account

Learn how to invite new users to access a client account on the UniRadio platform.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Access "Clients"

In the side menu, click on **"Clients"** to view the list of registered clients.

![Clients](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/invite-user/01-clients.jpeg)

### 3. Select the clinic

Click on the clinic to open its management page.

![Select clinic](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/invite-user/02-select-clinic.jpeg)

### 4. Access "Account Users"

Inside the client profile, click on **"Account Users"** to manage the users linked to it.

![Account Users](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/invite-user/03-users-account.jpeg)

### 5. Click on "Invite User"

Click the **"Invite User"** button to open the invitation form and fill in the new user's details.

:::tip User role
When inviting a user, you can define their role:

- **Administrative**: full access to the client account.
- **Member**: limited access. When selecting this role, you can configure the user's specific permissions.
  :::

:::tip New user
If the provided email address is not yet registered on the platform, the user will be created automatically upon accepting the invitation.
:::

:::warning Skip email sending
If the **"Skip invitation email"** option is checked, the invited user **will not receive any email notification** and will be activated automatically.
:::

![Invite User](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/invite-user/04-invite-user.jpeg)

### 6. Click "Send invitation"

After filling in the details, click **"Send invitation"** to complete the process.

![Send invitation](/images/en/radiologists/client-users/invite-user/05-send-invite.jpeg)
