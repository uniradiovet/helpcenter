# How to Delete a Webhook

Learn how to remove a webhook from your account on the UniRadio platform.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Open the settings menu

In the side menu, click the settings icon to open the account options.

![Settings menu](/images/en/radiologists/integrations/webhooks-delete/01-menu.jpeg)

### 3. Click "Integrations"

In the settings menu, click **"Integrations"** to access the integration options.

![Integrations](/images/en/radiologists/integrations/webhooks-delete/02-integrations.jpeg)

### 4. Click "Webhooks"

Inside integrations, click **"Webhooks"** to view the registered webhooks.

![Webhooks](/images/en/radiologists/integrations/webhooks-delete/03-webhooks.jpeg)

### 5. Click the trash icon to delete the desired webhook

![Delete webhook](/images/en/radiologists/integrations/webhooks-delete/04-delete.jpeg)

### 6. Click "Continue" to confirm the deletion

![Confirm deletion](/images/en/radiologists/integrations/webhooks-delete/05-confirm.jpeg)
