# How to Invite a New User to the Account

Learn how to grant access to your team by sending invitations through the settings area of the UniRadio platform.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Open the settings menu

In the side menu, click the settings icon to open the account options.

![Settings menu](/images/en/radiologists/users/invite-account-user/01-menu.jpeg)

### 3. Click "Users"

In the settings menu, click **"Users"** to manage the members of your account.

![Users](/images/en/radiologists/users/invite-account-user/02-users.jpeg)

### 4. Click "Invite User"

Click the **"Invite User"** button to start the invitation process.

![Invite User](/images/en/radiologists/users/invite-account-user/03-invite-user.jpeg)

### 5. Fill in the user information

Enter the name, email, and other required information for the invitation.

![Fill in information](/images/en/radiologists/users/invite-account-user/04-fill-info.jpeg)

### 6. Click "Send invitation"

After filling in the details, click **"Send invitation"** to finalize and send the invitation to the new user.

![Send invitation](/images/en/radiologists/users/invite-account-user/05-send-invite.jpeg)
