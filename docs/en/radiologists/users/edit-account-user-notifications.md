# How to Edit Account User Notifications

Learn how to adjust the notification preferences for a user in your account on the UniRadio platform.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Open the settings menu

In the side menu, click the settings icon to open the account options.

![Settings menu](/images/en/radiologists/users/edit-account-user-notifications/01-menu.jpeg)

### 3. Click "Users"

In the settings menu, click **"Users"** to view the members of your account.

![Users](/images/en/radiologists/users/edit-account-user-notifications/02-users.jpeg)

### 4. Select the user

Click the name of the user whose notifications you want to configure.

![Select user](/images/en/radiologists/users/edit-account-user-notifications/03-select-user.jpeg)

### 5. Click "Notifications"

On the user's page, click the **"Notifications"** tab to access the alert preferences.

![Notifications](/images/en/radiologists/users/edit-account-user-notifications/04-notifications.jpeg)

### 6. Select notification preferences

Check or uncheck the notification options as desired, such as alerts for new exams, issued reports, and other account activities.

![Notification preferences](/images/en/radiologists/users/edit-account-user-notifications/05-preferencias-notificacao.jpeg)

### 7. Click "Save"

After adjusting the settings, click **"Save"** to confirm the changes.

![Save](/images/en/radiologists/users/edit-account-user-notifications/06-save.jpeg)
