# For Radiologists

Welcome to the UniRadio documentation for radiologists! This guide will help you master all the features available to manage your radiology practice.

## What You Can Do

As a radiologist on UniRadio, you can:

- 👤 **Manage Your Profile** - Configure your professional information, subscription, and preferences
- 🏥 **Client Management** - Register and manage your client clinics
- 👥 **Team Collaboration** - Add team members and manage user permissions
- 📋 **Report Creation** - Issue, edit, and manage radiology reports
- 🖼️ **PACS Integration** - Connect your imaging equipment for an integrated workflow
- ⚙️ **Customization** - Configure report templates, logos, and system settings

## Getting Started

1. [Configure your profile](/en/radiologists/my-profile) with your professional information
2. [Register your first client](/radiologists/clients/register-client)
3. [Create report templates](/radiologists/report/create-report-templates) to speed up your workflow
4. [Configure PACS integration](/radiologists/pacs/pacs-settings) if you have digital imaging equipment
