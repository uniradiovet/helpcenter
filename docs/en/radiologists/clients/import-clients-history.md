# How to View Previous Client Imports

Learn how to access your client import history in UniRadio to review past uploads and manage account records.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Open the Clients section

Click **"Clients"** in the navigation menu to open your client account list.

![Clients Section](/images/en/radiologists/clients/import-clients-history/01-clientes.jpeg)

### 3. Open the options menu

Click the options button (dropdown) to display available actions for the client list.

![Toggle Dropdown](/images/en/radiologists/clients/import-clients-history/02-dropdown.jpeg)

### 4. Click "Import Clients"

Select **"Import Clients"** in the menu to open the import panel.

![Import Clients](/images/en/radiologists/clients/import-clients-history/03-importar-clientes.jpeg)

### 5. Click "View previous imports"

Click **"View previous imports"** to open the history page.

![View previous imports](/images/en/radiologists/clients/import-clients-history/04-ver-anteriores.jpeg)

### 6. Review the import list

The list shows all previous imports with details such as date, status, and number of processed records.

![Import list](/images/en/radiologists/clients/import-clients-history/05-lista-importacoes.jpeg)
