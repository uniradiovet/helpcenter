# How to Merge Duplicate Client Accounts

Learn how to consolidate duplicate client accounts on the UniRadio platform, keeping a single profile with all data unified.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Open the client list

In the side menu, click **"Clients"** to view all registered accounts.

![Client list](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/01-clients.jpeg)

### 3. Select the duplicate accounts

Check the selection boxes for the accounts you want to merge.

:::warning
You must select **at least two accounts** to perform a merge.
:::

![Select accounts](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/02-select-accounts.jpeg)

### 4. Click the actions dropdown

With the accounts selected, click the bulk actions dropdown menu.

![Actions dropdown](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/03-dropdown.jpeg)

### 5. Select "Merge Selected"

From the menu, choose the **"Merge Selected"** option.

![Merge selected](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/04-merge-selecionados.jpeg)

### 6. Click "Execute Action"

Confirm the operation by clicking **"Execute Action"**.

![Execute action](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/05-executar-acao.jpeg)

### 7. Select the account you want to keep

Choose which account will be the primary account after the merge.

:::warning
The selected account will be kept. **All data from the other accounts will be transferred to it**, and duplicate records will be discarded. **This action is irreversible.**
:::

![Select primary account](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/06-select-account.jpeg)

### 8. Click "Merge Accounts"

![Merge accounts](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/07-merge-accounts.jpeg)

### 9. Click "Continue" to confirm

![Confirm merge](/images/en/radiologists/clients/merge-clients/08-continue.jpeg)
