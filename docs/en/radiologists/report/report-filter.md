# How to Filter Reports

Learn how to use the UniRadio platform filters to quickly and efficiently locate completed reports.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Sign in to your account and go to [app.uniradio.vet.br/exams](https://app.uniradio.vet.br/exams)

### 2. Click "Completed"

Filter exams by the **"Completed"** status to view finished reports.

![Completed](/images/en/radiologists/report/report-filter/01-finalizado.jpeg)

### 3. Click "Filter"

Open the available filters panel.

![Filter](/images/en/radiologists/report/report-filter/02-filtrar.jpeg)

### 4. Select the desired filters

Choose the filtering criteria, such as date range, client, species, exam type, and others.

![Select filters](/images/en/radiologists/report/report-filter/03-select-filters.jpeg)

::: tip Available fields

- **Requesting Physician** — name of the physician who requested the exam.
- **Guardian** — name of the animal's owner.
- **Patient Name** — name of the animal.
- **Keywords** — terms present in the report or exam.
- **Radiologist** — name of the radiologist responsible for the report.
- **Species** — species of the animal (e.g., Canine, Feline).
- **Clinical suspicion** — diagnostic hypothesis provided when submitting the exam.
- **Region** — anatomical region examined (e.g., Abdomen, Thorax).
- **Tags** — labels assigned to the report.
- **Client** — name of the client clinic or institution.
- **Type** — exam modality (e.g., Radiography, Ultrasound).
- **Exam category** — classification assigned to the exam.
- **From (exam submission)** — start date of the period when the exam was submitted to the platform.
- **To (exam submission)** — end date of the period when the exam was submitted to the platform.
  :::

### 5. Click "Filter" to apply

Reports will be listed according to the selected filters.

![Apply filters](/images/en/radiologists/report/report-filter/04-apply-filters.jpeg)
