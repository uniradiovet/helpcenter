# How to Edit My Profile

Your profile contains your professional information that will appear on reports and communications.

## Step by Step

### 1. Access the UniRadio platform

Log in to your account at [app.uniradio.vet.br](https://app.uniradio.vet.br)

### 2. Select the "My Account" option

Click on the "My Account" menu to access settings.

![My Account](/images/radiologistas/minha-conta.png)

### 3. Go to the profile page

You will be redirected to the profile page. Click on "Edit profile".

![Edit Profile](/images/radiologistas/editar-perfil.png)

### 4. Fill out the form

Complete the form with your information and click "Save Changes".

![Profile Form](/images/radiologistas/formulario-perfil.png)

## Field Descriptions

### Professional Identity

**Change logo**
- Allows you to change the account logo
- This logo will appear on your reports
- Recommended size: 300x300 pixels
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG

**Name**
- Full name of the professional or account holder
- This name will appear on all reports you generate

**Tax ID (CNPJ)**
- National Registry of Legal Entities
- Fill this field if you are a legal entity
- Format: 00.000.000/0000-00

### Academic Information

**Lattes Curriculum**
- Link to the professional's Lattes Curriculum, if available
- Optional, but adds credibility

**Website**
- Address of professional or institutional website, if any
- Include the full URL (https://...)

### Technical Responsibility

**Technical responsible and CRMV**
- Full name of the technical responsible
- Registration number with the Veterinary Medicine Council
- Fill if applicable

### Digital Signature

**Digital signature**
- Area to draw or upload the digital signature
- Will be used on reports
- Make sure it is clear and professional

**Professional**
- Name of the professional responsible for issuing reports

**CRMV**
- Registration number with the Regional Council of Veterinary Medicine

### Professional Details

**Description**
- Brief summary about experience or professional practice
- Specialties and areas of expertise

**Professional phone**
- Phone number for professional contact
- Format: (00) 00000-0000

**Professional email**
- Email address used for professional communications
- May be different from the login email

### Address Information

**Address**
- Complete location information:
  - ZIP code
  - Street
  - Number
  - State
  - City

### System Preferences

**Notifications**
- Option to enable or disable notifications about new exams
- Recommended: keep enabled to stay updated

**Interface language**
- Selection of the desired language for platform navigation
- Options: Portuguese, English, Spanish

**Timezone**
- Local timezone configuration for correct display of dates and times

## Important Tips

::: tip Professional Image
Your logo and signature are important for your professional image. Make sure they are:
- High quality
- Clear and readable
- Professional in appearance
:::

::: warning Required Fields
Fields marked with * are required and must be filled in to save your profile.
:::
