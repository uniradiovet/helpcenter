# How to Create an Exam

This guide will show you how to send a new exam to UniRadio for radiological analysis.

## Step by Step

### 1. Log in to the UniRadio App

Access your account at [app.uniradio.vet.br](https://app.uniradio.vet.br)

### 2. Click "New Exam"

On the main panel, click the "New Exam" button.

![New Exam Button](/images/clientes/novo-exame.png)

### 3. Choose the Upload Method

Select the "File upload" option.

![File Upload Option](/images/clientes/upload-arquivos.png)

### 4. Upload the Images

Select the exam images:
- By clicking the "Select files" button
- Or by dragging them into the dotted area

![Upload Area](/images/clientes/area-upload.png)

::: info Supported Formats
- **Image Formats**: JPEG, PNG, DICOM
- **Maximum Size**: 120 MB combined
:::

### 5. Add Image Links (Optional)

If you prefer to share a link to where the images are saved (Dropbox, Google Drive), use the "Link to images" field.

![Image Link Field](/images/clientes/link-imagens.png)

### 6. Click "Save and Continue"

After uploading or adding links, click "Save and continue".

![Save and Continue](/images/clientes/salvar-continuar.png)

### 7. Fill in the Exam Information

Complete the form with all required information:

![Exam Form](/images/clientes/formulario-exame.png)

#### Required Fields (marked with *)

**Client Section:**
- **Client**: Select your clinic from the dropdown menu

**Exam Data:**
- **Exam Type**: Choose the modality (Radiography, CT, Ultrasound, etc.)
- **Regions**: Select the anatomical regions to be examined
- **Requesting Veterinarian**: Full name of the responsible veterinarian
- **Exam Date**: Date the exam was performed

**Patient Data:**
- **Patient Name**: Animal's name
- **Species**: Patient species (Canine, Feline, etc.)
- **Breed**: Patient breed
- **Weight**: Patient weight
- **Age**: Patient age
- **Age in**: Unit (years or months)
- **Sex**: Patient sex

**Clinical History:**
- **Clinical History**: Relevant medical history of the patient
- **Clinical Signs**: Current symptoms or signs
- **Clinical Suspicion**: Suspected or differential diagnosis

### 8. Submit the Exam

After filling in all information, click "Submit exam".

![Submit Exam](/images/clientes/enviar-exame.png)

### 9. Confirmation

You will see a success message: "Exam updated successfully!"

![Success Message](/images/clientes/mensagem-sucesso.png)

### 10. Verify the Submission

Go to the Exams page and check if your exam is in the "Awaiting" list to be reported.

![Exam List](/images/clientes/lista-exames.png)

## Field Guide

### Exam Type Options
- Radiography
- Computed Tomography (CT)
- Magnetic Resonance (MR)
- Ultrasound
- Electrocardiography
- Echocardiography
- Endoscopy

### Region Selection

Choose from anatomical regions:
- **Thoracic Limb** (Left/Right) - proximal, middle or distal
- **Pelvic Limb** (Left/Right) - proximal, middle or distal
- **Axial Skeleton** - skull, spine, coxal, thorax, abdomen, etc.

### Exams with Contrast

If using contrast:
1. Check "Exam with Contrast"
2. Select the region where contrast will be applied
3. Specify details if necessary

### Optional Fields

**Previous Exam**
- Attach or reference related previous exams

**Owner Information**
- Owner name
- Owner email (will receive report notifications if provided)

**Exam Considerations**
- Relevant observations during the exam
- Example: "Restless animal"

## Important Tips

::: tip Image Quality
- Make sure images are clear and well-lit
- Include all relevant views
- Remove patient identification information if necessary
:::

::: tip Complete Information
- Provide as much clinical history as possible
- Detailed information helps radiologists provide better reports
:::

::: warning File Names
Avoid special characters, accents, or parentheses in image file names. This may prevent images from displaying correctly.
:::

## What Happens Next?

1. Your exam is submitted and enters the "Awaiting" queue
2. A radiologist will review and accept the exam
3. The radiologist will generate a report
4. You will receive an email notification when the report is ready
5. Access your report in the "Completed" section

## Troubleshooting

### Images Won't Upload
- Check file size (max. 120 MB total)
- Check file format (JPEG, PNG or DICOM)
- Check your internet connection

### Form Won't Submit
- Make sure all required fields (*) are filled
- Verify that at least one image or link was provided
- Try refreshing the page and try again

## Alternative: Send via PACS

If you have digital imaging equipment, you can send exams directly via PACS integration. See our [PACS sending guide](/en/clientes/enviar-pacs).
