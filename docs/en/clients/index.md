# For Clients

Welcome to the UniRadio documentation for clients! This guide will help you send exams and access your radiology reports.

## What You Can Do

As a client on UniRadio, you can:

- 📤 **Send Exams** - Upload images and exam information for analysis
- 🖼️ **PACS Integration** - Send exams directly from your imaging equipment
- 📋 **Access Reports** - View and download your radiology reports
- 👤 **Profile Management** - Update your clinic information
- ✏️ **Edit Exams** - Modify exam details before analysis

## Getting Started

1. [Configure your profile](/clients/account/my-profile)
2. [Send your first exam](/clients/exam/exam-new)
